The DeviceTimestamp is a device clock in microseconds with near-constant step (~16667 µs).

The file: timeseries.csv contains the following columns
SubjectID
DeviceTimestamp (see comment above)
CycleID: Corresponds to the trial (1-10 trials per subject)
Phase: Corresponds to either Baseline, Relax(ation), Break
PupilDiameter: in mm
GazeX	
GazeY	
GazeZ	
PulseBPM: Heart rate in bpm
PPG_SQI: Signal Quality of heart rate
MotionMag: Magnitude of motion detected
